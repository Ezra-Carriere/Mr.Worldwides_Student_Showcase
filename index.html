Interactive Media Design Program

The Interactive Media Design program will professionally prepare students for an evolving job market in digital media design, development and production, providing comprehensive training in new media technologies with an emphasis on web application design, interactive rich media, and current technologies for developing usable, media-rich sites and applications. The curriculum will combine courses in design, coding, marketing and media management and incorporate industry-standard tools from companies such as Adobe and Maxon, as well as open-source technologies for web application development.

Level Four Client Project

Level Four Client Project

As they reach the critical Level Four juncture in our Interactive Media Design program, students are thrust into the heart of the media industry. Engaging directly with renowned media corporations, they undertake projects in areas like graphic design, 3D animation, UI/UX, and comprehensive web development. Each project serves as a real-world test of their skills, accompanied by invaluable client feedback. Beyond the learning curve, it's a golden ticket for many: outstanding work can pave the way to full-time roles within these industry-leading firms, providing a seamless transition from academic achievement to professional success.
